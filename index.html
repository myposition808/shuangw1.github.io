Research Focus

I got a B.E.degree in Architecture & Conservation from Tongji University, Shanghai, China, and my MSc. of Historic Preservation from University of Pennsylvania, School of Design.

I am now a PhD candidate in Built Environment at University of Washington. My research interest is resilience of socio-ecological systems and cultural landscape preservation. I have work experience as an architect intern in Brenne Architect, Berlin, Germany and I have worked in PennDesign as a research associate on the vulnerability study of Fort Union National Monument in New Mexico under climate change.
